Sword of Ember

A legendary blade forged in the heart of a volcano, its fiery enchantment burns enemies to ash.

Home / Items / Sword of Ember

Weapon Overview

Base Stats

  • Damage: High
  • Attack Speed: Medium
  • Range: Melee
  • Durability: Legendary

Special Properties

  • Element: Fire
  • Enchantment: Burning
  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Level Requirement: 15

Fire Damage Bonuses

Primary Fire Enhancement

+20% Fire Damage to all attacks

This bonus applies to both basic attacks and fire-based abilities, making it an excellent choice for fire mages and pyromancers.

Burn Effect Application

Attacks apply 1 Burn stack (maximum 5 stacks)

Each successful hit with the Sword of Ember applies a burn stack that deals damage over time. Multiple stacks increase the damage exponentially.

Synergies & Combinations

Arcane Talisman Synergy

When equipped together, the Arcane Talisman provides +10% burn duration, extending the effectiveness of your fire damage.

+10% Burn Duration Magical Enhancement

Fire Mastery Build

Combine with other fire-enchanted items to create a devastating fire-based character build.

  • Fire Robes for additional fire damage
  • Flame Rings for burn effect enhancement
  • Phoenix Feather for fire resistance

Acquisition & Crafting

How to Obtain

  • Dungeon Drop: Rare drop from fire-themed dungeons
  • Boss Reward: Defeat fire elementals or volcanic bosses
  • Crafting: Requires rare materials and high-level blacksmithing
  • Quest Reward: Complete the "Flame Forge" questline

Required Materials

  • Volcanic Steel: Mined from active volcanoes
  • Phoenix Essence: Rare drop from phoenix creatures
  • Fire Crystal: Found in fire-themed dungeons
  • Ancient Runes: Decoded from ancient tablets

Combat Strategy

The Sword of Ember excels in sustained combat where you can build up burn stacks and maintain fire damage pressure.

Best Against

  • Ice-based enemies (fire weakness)
  • High-health bosses (burn stacks)
  • Groups of enemies (area burn)
  • Armored foes (fire penetration)

Weak Against

  • Fire-resistant enemies
  • Fast-moving targets
  • Water-based creatures
  • Enemies with fire immunity
