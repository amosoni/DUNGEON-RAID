Item Categories

Dungeon Raid features a diverse array of equipment that can be categorized by their primary function and rarity.

Weapons

Primary damage dealers that determine your attack style and damage output.

  • Swords: Balanced weapons with good damage and attack speed
  • Axes: High damage weapons with slower attack speed
  • Bows: Ranged weapons for tactical positioning
  • Staffs: Magical weapons that enhance spell power

Armor & Defense

Protective equipment that increases survivability and provides special resistances.

  • Light Armor: High mobility with moderate protection
  • Medium Armor: Balanced defense and mobility
  • Heavy Armor: Maximum protection with reduced mobility
  • Magical Robes: Spell resistance and mana enhancement

Artifacts & Accessories

Magical items that provide unique abilities and stat bonuses.

  • Rings: Provide various stat bonuses and effects
  • Amulets: Powerful magical enhancements
  • Belts: Utility items with special abilities
  • Cloaks: Defensive items with unique properties
