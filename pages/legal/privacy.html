Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data while providing the best gaming experience.

Data Collection Overview

We are committed to transparency about what data we collect and how we use it. Our approach is to collect only what's necessary for providing and improving our service.

What We Collect

  • Analytics Data: Basic usage statistics to improve our service
  • Error Logs: Technical information to fix bugs and issues
  • Game Performance: Anonymous data to optimize gameplay
  • Device Information: Basic technical details for compatibility

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or addresses
  • Financial Data: No payment or banking information
  • Location Data: No GPS or location tracking
  • Biometric Data: No fingerprints or facial recognition

Analytics & Performance

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how our website is used and to improve user experience. This service may set cookies and collect anonymous usage data.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing browser extensions or adjusting your browser settings.

Performance Monitoring

We monitor game performance and website functionality to ensure smooth gameplay and identify areas for improvement.

This includes anonymous data about loading times, error rates, and user interactions.

Third-Party Services

Game Embedding

Our game is embedded from MiniPlay.com. This service may set cookies and collect data according to their own privacy policy.

External Service Separate Policy

External Links

Our website contains links to external services like Steam. These services have their own privacy policies and data collection practices.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external services you use.

Data Protection & Security

Security Measures

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted
  • Secure Hosting: Professional hosting with security measures
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates
  • Access Control: Limited access to collected data

Data Retention

We retain analytics and error log data for a limited time to improve our service. This data is automatically deleted after 12 months.

No personal data is stored long-term, and all data is anonymized where possible.

Your Rights & Choices

Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, though this may affect website functionality.

Data Access

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, we're happy to answer questions about our data practices.

Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of analytics tracking and limit data collection through browser settings and privacy tools.

Contact & Support

Questions About Privacy

support@dungeonraid.online

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We'll notify users of significant changes through our website.
