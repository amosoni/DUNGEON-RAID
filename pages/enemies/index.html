Enemy Types

Dungeon Raid features various enemy types, each with unique abilities and attack patterns. Understanding these is crucial for survival.

Basic Enemies

Standard dungeon denizens that appear in groups. They're relatively weak individually but dangerous in numbers.

  • Goblins: Fast, weak enemies that attack in swarms
  • Skeletons: Undead warriors with moderate health and damage
  • Orcs: Tough melee fighters with high health pools

Elite Enemies

Stronger variants with special abilities. These require tactical thinking and proper resource management.

  • Goblin King: Elite boss with powerful area attacks
  • Dark Mages: Spellcasters that can heal allies
  • Armored Knights: High defense units that require special tactics

Boss Enemies

The ultimate challenges that test your mastery of the game's mechanics.

  • Dungeon Lords: Final bosses with multiple phases
  • Ancient Dragons: Legendary creatures with devastating abilities
  • Corrupted Guardians: Corrupted protectors of ancient treasures
