Abilities & Attacks

War Cry

The Goblin King lets out a terrifying battle cry that buffs all nearby goblins, increasing their damage and attack speed.

Area Effect Buff Allies

Cleave

A powerful sweeping attack that deals massive damage in a wide arc. This is his primary damage-dealing ability.

High Damage Wide Range

Shield Bash

A defensive ability that stuns the player and provides the Goblin King with temporary armor bonus.

Stun Effect Armor Bonus

Weaknesses & Strategy

Primary Weaknesses

  • Stun/Freeze: Vulnerable to crowd control effects
  • Fire Damage: Takes increased damage from fire-based attacks
  • Single Target: Struggles against multiple simultaneous threats

Recommended Strategy

  1. Bait the Cleave: Position yourself to make him waste his powerful attack
  2. Stun Timing: Use stun effects when he's about to use War Cry
  3. Focus Fire: Concentrate damage during vulnerable windows
  4. Resource Management: Save healing items for after Shield Bash

Loot & Rewards

Defeating the Goblin King grants valuable rewards and unlocks new content.

  • Experience: Large XP boost for character progression
  • Gold: Substantial gold reward for equipment purchases
  • Rare Items: Chance to drop powerful equipment
  • Progression: Unlocks access to deeper dungeon levels
