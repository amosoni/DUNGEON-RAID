Quick Start

  • Match 3+ swords to attack, shields to gain armor, potions to heal, mana for spells.
  • Prioritize survival at low HP; clear threats before greed matches.
  • Look two moves ahead; set up cascades, then trigger with high-value tiles.

First Build Tips

Stack early defense, then add damage multipliers. Save ultimates for elites/bosses.
